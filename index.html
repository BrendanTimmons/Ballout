Ballout

Controls

  • Move Player: Left / Right
  • Speed Boost: Shift
  • Pause: Space
  • Quit: Esc

Gameplay

  • Destroy the blocks without missing the ball.
  • Move your ship to deflect the ball.
  • The ball speeds up every time it hits an object.
  • Increase your score by killing blocks (20 points) and increase your combo multiplier by 1 for every block you hit in a row. When the ball hits the player ship the combo multiplier is reset.
